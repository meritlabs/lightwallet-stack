How do I grow my Community?

When you bring an active new member into the Merit community, your growth score will increase. There is a growth lottery every minute, and a higher score earns you a higher chance to win. To grow your community, you can:

  • Tell your friends your share code:
  • Share a summary of Merit with friends! Copy a summary to your clipboard
  • Tell your friends and family in person! Your alias is your invite code.
  • Have a new idea for how to share? Let us Know!

How do I get Growth Rewards?

  • The best way to win Growth Mining Rewards is to increase your growth score. You can do this by sending your invite tokens to friends, family or colleagues that would be a good addition to the Merit community.
  • Growth Mining is not just about the number of people you invite; it's also about how active those people are. Keep this in mind as you use your limited number of invites.

How to get Security Rewards?

  • Security mining requires a desktop computer. You can mine using the full-node Merit wallet or the Merit Standalone miner. Get started by downloading the Merit wallet and importing your wallet using your 12-word passphrase. You can read more about how to sync your wallet.
  • Because there are many security miners in the Merit community, most users will want to join a mining pool. Merit offers a free zero-fee pool that allows you to mine along with other members of the community, increasing your chances of winning security rewards.
