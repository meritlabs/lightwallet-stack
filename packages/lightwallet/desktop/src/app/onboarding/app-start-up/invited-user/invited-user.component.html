Create your first wallet

If you know about Merit from your friend, and you already have person who can accept your invite into Merit World, you can start using your Merit Web Wallet in couple clicks

Before you start, we recomend finish reading this short tutorial.

After that you will press "Create Wallet" you will see screen with two fields: "Invite code or username" and "Your username (alias)"

* Be careful when you createing your Alias (user name) because it can be never changed.

Your alias it's your invite code if you will decide to invite somebody into Merit
