Send Merit any way you want!

You can send Merit any way you want! To a contact in your phone book, to your favorite chat or social media, through a shareable link. To an existing user, through their Merit alias or address.

Send Merit to anyone!

You can send Merit to anyone, whether they are on Merit or not. Because merit is invite only. When sending Merit to new user, an invite will automatically be sent along with it. This means you’ll need at least one invite to bring somebody into the community.

Don’t worry!

Don’t worry. When sending to a user that’s not on Merit, you have full control over your transaction. You can cancel an transaction anytime until the funds have been claimed. You can add a password to the transaction to be extra sure that it goes to the intended recipient.
